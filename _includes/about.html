About

Umich Soccer Project Team focuses on how to combine data processing with soccer to bring more professional analysis to the UMich soccer team. Aubree Xia, Colin Badgero, David Lee, Nikhil Satpathy, Selina Rey and Simon Vasquez are students in this research team. Albert Berahas and Raed AL Kontar are initiators and principal responsible persons of this research.

If you want to know more about our analysis, please feel free to contact us!
